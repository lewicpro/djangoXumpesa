Preparing Your Return By Mail

Small Parcel (I.e. FedEx) Returns

Detach the top of the invoice and keep it for your records. Pack the item(s) securely in the original packaging. Complete the return reason questionnaire on the original packing slip and place it inside the box. If your shipping receipt included a pre-paid return label, please affix it on to your return package. Before sending your return shipment, please remove all extra labels from the outside of the package. Many of the orders will include a prepaid UPS return label. If using the pre-paid return label, please follow steps 1-5 directly above and proceed to drop off your package at any UPS drop off location. No postage is necessary; $6.50 will be deducted from your return credit for use of the label. If you would prefer to use your own shipping method instead of the pre-paid option on the packing slip or the label was not included on your packing ship, please follow steps 1-3 above and contact XaMarket Customer Care at 0719060504 for help facilitating your return to the returns processing center.

Freight Returns
If you are not satisfied with the purchase, and the item was originally delivered by one of our oversized freight companies, please call the Xamarket Customer Care Team at 0719060504 for help with your oversized return.

Returns Exceptions

  • Items designated as "Online Only" cannot be returned to a XaMarket store location. They must be shipped to the XaMarket Returns Department prepaid and insured by the customer. This information is detailed in Returning an Item and is also printed out and included with your order.
  • Shipping Expenses are non-refundable unless the return is a result of a manufacturing defect or our shipping error. If such an event occurs, please contact World Market Customer Care immediately to arrange the return and we will refund all shipping expenses.
  • Gift Cards are not returnable.
  • Gift Wrap fees are non-refundable, unless an item is damaged or defective.
  • Items purchased from a XaMarket retail store cannot be returned by mail.
  • Returns to a XaMarket store location after 60 days of purchase will be issued store credit.
